Rules

Points are awarded for:

  • Goals scored (+5 points)
  • Assists (+3 points)
  • Clean sheets (for defenders and goalkeepers) (+4 points)
  • Saves (for goalkeepers) (+1 point per save)
  • Yellow cards (-1 point)
  • Red cards (-3 points)
